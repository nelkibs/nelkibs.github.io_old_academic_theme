About Me

Nelson Kibinge is a Senior Research Associate with the genetic epidemiology group at the MRC Integrated Epidemiology Unit at the university of Bristol. His research interests include statistical genomics of autoimmune diseases, malaria immunology and virus genetic diversity. He has been leading the statistics department at the KEMRI-Wellcome Trust, which conducts extensive clinical trials and pathogen surveillance studies. Nelson has led the development of statistical and computational systems for analysis of the resultant data from these studies. Nelson also has an interest in human genetics and variation.
