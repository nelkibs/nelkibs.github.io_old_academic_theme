Nelson Kibinge

Senior Postdoctoral Researcher

University of Bristol

KEMRI-Wellcome Trust

Nelson Kibinge is a senior research associate at the University of Bristol (MRC-IEU). His interests include development of statistical and computational biology applications for genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics and metabolomics data mining. He is involved in diverse research areas including autoimmune diseases, malaria and respiratory virus epidemiology with experience in biomarker and therapeutic target prioritization. Nelson is also affiliated to the KEMRI-Wellcome Trust Research Program, a unique center of scientific research excellence in Africa and is also a part-time lecturer at the Pwani University department of Biochemistry.
